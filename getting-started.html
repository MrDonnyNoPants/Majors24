GT3 CLASS START HERE 2026 GUIDE
New to Majors 24? Follow these steps before you race.

1 What This Page Is For

This page is a simple, practical onboarding guide for every driver and team contact. It focuses on the first actions that need to happen immediately after registration and before race day.

1
Confirm your place on the team in iRacing. Make sure you are listed on the team roster in the iRacing team page for Majors 24.
2
Make a free account at majorsseries.com. Visit https://majorsseries.com/register and complete the registration.
3
Join the Majors 24/MEC League from the iRacing UI. League ID: 9686. League name currently shows as: Majors 24
4
Inform the team owner or Michael Sylvester. Tell Stiaan Neveling or Michael Sylvester so you can be added to the Majors 24 team.

2 Practice Sessions

Once approved for the Majors 24 league, both practice and Pre-Qualifying sessions appear in iRacing under Leagues → Up Next.

  1. Look for Majors 24 practice and Pre-Q sessions under Leagues: Up Next in iRacing.
  2. These sessions become available starting May 30, 2026 at 2:00 AM CDT. Every driver must set at least one PQ lap before the deadline or they cannot race.
ℹ All times are shown in your local timezone. The practice session time above updates to match your system clock.
